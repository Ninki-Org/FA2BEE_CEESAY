Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our couch cleaning services.

How long does the cleaning process take?

The duration depends on the size of your furniture and the cleaning method. Typically, a standard sofa takes 2-3 hours to clean and dry completely.

Do you clean on-site or pick up furniture?

We offer both options! You can choose our on-site cleaning service where we come to your home, or our pickup & delivery service for added convenience.

Can you remove tough stains and odors?

Yes! Our specialized stain and odor removal treatments can handle most common issues including wine spills, pet stains, and food marks. We assess each stain individually to determine the best approach.

Is the cleaning safe for all fabrics?

Our cleaning solutions are eco-friendly and safe for most fabrics. Before starting, we test a small, hidden area to ensure compatibility. We also offer fabric protection treatments to extend the life of your furniture.
